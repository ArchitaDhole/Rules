Instructions to Parents

  1. Parents/Guardians are requested to pay regular attention to the ward's assignments at home.
  2. Parents Guardians are expected to sign remarks, periodica reports, result cards or any other similar documents. Failure or refusal to sign render the students liable to be sent home.
  3. Repeated late coming or forgetting to bring books or not doing the homework may be punished.
  4. If you feel that your ward does not make desired progress, the Principal may be contacted. Private tutions from class/subject teachers should not be engaged without consulting the Principal.
  5. Sickness of the ward concerned should be promptly reported as early as possible and leave be asked for, on submission of medical certificate.
  6. Fees are payable as long as pupil's name remains on the roll.
  7. Habitual late coming, irregular attendance, insubordination, serious misbehaviour, obscenity in any form, dishonesty, theft, frequent failure, any one of these circumstances may justify dismissal. Normally previous warnings will be issued.
  8. In case of any query about the ward, parents can see the concemed teachers on the month-end day after 5th period.
  9. Parents should not approach the class directly. They are requested to inform the office first about the concerned.
  10. Parents / Guardians are specially requested to inform the school about any change in their address or telephone number as early as possible.

Rules And Discipline

  1. Generally no child will remain absent from school without pre-consent of the Principal. Incase he/she has to remain absent on account of any unavoidable circumstances he/she must produce a written statement by the guardian mentioning the reason of absence.
  2. Pupil's absence for more than a week, habitual idleness, disobedience, and misbehaviour will be liable for dismissal.
  3. Any kind of damage done by the pupil in the school premises must be made repaired.
  4. During the absence of the teacher, the class monitor is responsible for maintaining order and discipline in the class.
  5. Silence must be observed in the school premises.
  6. Every student must attend the school in time and in uniform regularly.
  7. The pupil must obtain aggregate 40% and minimum 35% marks in each subject for passing.
  8. Valuable things should not be brought in the class. The school will not be responsible for the loss of them.
  9. Personal and common cleanliness and hygiene must strictly be observed by all.
  10. In case of undisciplined behavior and failure consecutively for two years in the same standard, the pupil will be dismissed.
  11. No child will leave the school without the permission of the Principal.
  12. Every student must bring the school diary in the class every day.

Rules And Regulations

  1. The certificate of birth will have to be produced at the time of admission for Nursery section.
  2. A pupil coming from another recognised school will be admitted on producing the school leaving certificate issued by the previous school. In case of candidate coming from school out side Maharashtra State the certificate must be countersigned by the inspector or education officer concerned.
  3. No leaving certificate will be issued unless the parents make a request in writing on a prescribed printed application available in the office.
  4. If the student is withdrawn in October / April, the fees till November/May must be paid respectively.
  5. A leaving certificate will not be issued unless all fees and dues have been paid in full.
  6. Fees must be paid in advance before the 15th of each month. A child who has not paid all fees may be barred from examination.

Library

  1. Students are not allowed to keep any borrowed book from the school library for more than 4 days without permission, else a fine of Rs. 10/- per day will be collected from such students.
  2. If a book is lost or damaged the students will have to make either the loss or damage.
  3. It is strictly forbidden to write anything or any remarks in the book or underline any words. If any such things are noticed in the book before borrowing it they should be shown to librarian.
  4. No exchange of books among the students themselves be allowed.
  5. All library books should be neatly and carefully handled.
